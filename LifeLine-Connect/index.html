Welcome to the LIFE Line Help page! Here, you can learn all the ins and outs of this fantastic tool to keep connected. LIFE Line is a one-stop, convenient way to keep informed about the latest recognition and upcoming promotions or events, or to share a unique experience. Below are a few helpful hints that are sure to make your LIFE Line experience a great one!

Creating/Sending a New Message

  • As a new LIFE Member and LIFE Line customer you have the ability to send a message to your upline 6,000 for 6 Trip Qualified Member.

  • To create and send a voice message, press the “Send a Message” button on the home page.

  • You may add a text tag to your message by typing in the box that includes the words “Message tag”.

  • To record your message, press the “Start” button. Then press “Stop” when finished. The timer in the bottom right shows how much time you have remaining for that message. Press “Play” to review what you’ve just recorded. If you wish to discard that message and record again, simply press “Start” and repeat the process. Press either “Send” to send the message or “Cancel” to abort the send message process altogether.

  • You message will be sent to your upline 6,000 for 6 Trip Qualifier. This person’s name will be listed under your Settings page.

Listening/Managing a Received Message

  • To listen to messages from your Upline, press the “from Upline” button. The number inside the red star shows you the number of new messages in your inbox. Simply select the message from the list of messages to listen to the message. The lighter shaded messages are messages that have not been listened to. The darker shaded messages have already been listen to. After playing the message you may Pause, Replay or Stop the message by selecting the appropriate button. You may go to the Previous or Next message by selecting either of those buttons.

  • You may Reply to the message with either Voice or Text by pressing “Reply to This Message” and choosing the” Voice” or “Text” option. If you choose “Text”, simply type your message in the box and press “Reply” or “Cancel” to cancel. If you choose “Voice”, press Start to record the message and then “Reply” or “Cancel” to cancel.

  • Clicking the “Tasks” menu at the top while viewing a specific message also gives you the “Reply” option as well as the option to “Archive” or “Delete” the message.

Archived Messages

  • To Archive a message that you have just listened to, choose “Tasks” then “Archive” from the Tasks menu.

  • To listen to an Archived message, press the “Archive” button from the home page then Play your selected message by simply choosing the message.

Settings Options

  • Press the “Settings” button in the upper right of the home page to view Settings. Displayed will be the username of the account in use, LIFE Line Help, Terms of Service, the Privacy Policy and My Settings.

  • Press “My Settings” to see the name of your upline 6,000 for 6 Trip qualifier, to turn on/off new message notifications or to take a profile picture that will be attached to your messages.

  • To take a picture, press “Take New Picture” and the camera will appear. Take a picture and either “Retake”, or “Use Photo” when you’re happy with the picture. Click Save Settings when complete.
